Australia Recruits Doctors that Ireland Struggles to Retain

Kelly Waldron

February 19, 2022

Between June 2021 and 2022, 405 visas were granted to Irish medical doctors by the Australian government, most of whom are under the age of 30. That is equivalent to almost half the number of people who became registered doctors from Irish medical schools last year.
